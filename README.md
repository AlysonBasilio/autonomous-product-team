# Autonomous Product Team

This document specifies how an autonomous product team should work.

## Definitions

### Team

The team which will be responsible for handleing a product.

### Team Manager

The persona that is responsible by manage team members and delegates tasks.

### Team Member

The persona that executes tasks.

### Tasks

The definition of how to do a specific activity to reach a specific goal.

## Premises

1. Each team have one team manager
2. A team can have as many team members as possible
3. Team Manager do not execute tasks. They will always delegate tasks for team members.
